Looking For Collaborators - Letia Subconscious

Created on March 26, 2026

2026   ·   collaboration,   open-source,   ai   ·   Projects

I am looking for collaborators for Letia Subconscious.

What this project is about

Letia Subconscious is a universal persistent memory layer for coding agents and AI assistants. The idea is simple: your AI should not forget everything between sessions. Instead of memory being locked inside one tool, Letia keeps your working context (decisions, preferences, unfinished threads, and past attempts) and injects it into whichever AI setup you use.

It is designed to work across models and IDEs, including Claude, GPT, Gemini, Copilot, and local models, as well as Cursor, Windsurf, VS Code, Zed, JetBrains, and more. The long-term goal is one shared subconscious that follows your workflow everywhere, so context survives across projects, tools, and sessions.

Project link: Psionic-labs/Letia-subconscious

If you are interested in contributing, feel free to reach out. I would love to build this with people who enjoy solving interesting problems.
